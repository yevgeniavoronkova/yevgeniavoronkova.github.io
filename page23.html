          Kharkiv Medical Academy of Post-graduate Education (KhMAPE) is a higher educational establishment of the IV-th level accreditation (certificate series РД – IV № 211770 of May,14 2005 according to the decision of the state accreditation commission of April, 19 2005, report №55), which realizes post-graduate education of specialists according to the direction «Medicine» (1201) – all specialities and « Pharmacy » (1202), and also realizes post-graduate education of doctors-specialists in internship, magistracy, aspirantura, clinical ordinatura and also doctors pass their training course in accordance with basis directions.

         License to the right of the realization of the educational activities of improvement of professional skills of doctors-foreigners (doctors, pharmaceuticals) from different countries is given by the Ministry of Education and science of Ukraine 07/11/2005 (series АБ №175951).

Educational activities are carried out on 72 departments united in 5 faculties:

Terapeutical;

Surgical;

Pediatrics;

Of general practice – family medicine;

Of preventive medicine.

         Academy utilizes for training of foreign specialists 100 lecture-halls, 250 lecture-rooms, library (with access to the Internet) 44 clinical laboratories and also 42 diagnostic laboratories of medical establishment.

         Academy also has Medical college which conducts training of foreign citizens on the base of the secondary education – junior specialists (nurse), on the base of the secondary special education (junior specialist) – bachelors (nurse).

         KhMAPE has 2 hostels wits 4 buildings where pupils and specialists can live. All foreign citizens, passing training at the Academy, are completely provided with habitation.
